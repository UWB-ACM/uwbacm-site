Our Recurring Events

These are our events that occur throughout the school year (September through June).
We have more events than what's listed here, if you're interested in learning more: Check out our events page!

Technical Interview Preparation

Prepare for technical interviews with real examples, experiences, and recruiters!

Competitive Programming

Compete with students across the West Coast to solve coding questions and win prizes!

UWBHacks Hackathon

Our annual UWBHacks hackathon takes place in Spring. Students work with cutting edge technology to gain experience and build amazing apps!

Technical Workshops

We alternate workshops throughout the year, teaching crucial industry tools such as Git and Linux.

Tech Panel & Networking Night

Tech panel with industry professionals that wraps up with a dinner and a chance to sit down and connect with Engineers and Managers from companies such as Boeing, T-Mobile, Microsoft, Amazon, and more!
