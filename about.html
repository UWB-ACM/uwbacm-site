What is ACM?

ACM is the largest international computing organization. Well stablished in the tech industry, it provide its members with diverse learning opportunities.

UWB ACM

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) Chapter at UW Bothell was first founded in 1997, and through each iteration, it has implemented great changes and improvements. The current chapter was re-established in Fall 2017, hoping to facilitate collaboration among students with a focus on fostering professional and educational development in the field of computer science.
